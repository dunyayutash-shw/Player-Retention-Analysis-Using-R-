# Player-Retention-Analysis-Using-R-
An R-based data analysis project exploring factors that influence player retention in online games through data cleaning, exploratory analysis and visualizations

## Introduction
Player retention is a key performance indicator for online gaming companies because retaining existing players is often cost-effective than acquiring new ones. Analyzing player retention data using R to identify patterns in player behavior by examining factors associated with player Engagement "while the dataset does not contain direct Retention measures, Engagement level is used as a proxy indicator of player activity and continued participation".

## objective 
finding key factors effecting player engagement and making insights that can support stategies improving player engagement and long-term retention.

## Dataset
[online_gaming_behavior_dataset.csv.zip](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/29810680/online_gaming_behavior_dataset.csv.zip)

## key findings 
-Players in USA had the highest engagement level , which were associated with better retention than players from other regions.
-Most players with high engagement rates have not made any investments in the game, suggesting an opportunity to increase revenue through targeted promotions.
-Games with Easy difficulty setting are played on a wider range.
-Retention varied accross player segments, suggesting opportunities for targeted engagement strategies.

## Recommendations 

- Develop targeted engagement campaigns for players outside the USA to improve their retention and participation.
- Encourage in-game purchases by offering personalized discounts or rewards to highly engaged players who have not yet made any invesments.
- Maintain and expand game modes with easy difficulty settinh, as they appear to attract broader range of players
- Create retention strategies tailored to different player segments, such as personalized events, loyalty rewards or achievement-based incentives.
- Monitor player engagement regularly to identify users at risk of becoming inactive  with targeted notifications or special offers.
